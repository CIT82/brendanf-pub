Vault-Tec Says You're SPECIAL!

  • John Smith
  • January 21, 2077
  • 12 Comments

Vault-Tec published a book written for children called "You're SPECIAL!" This book identifies the seven key attributes that are viewed as the most desirable in a Vault resident. The attributes are as follows in order: Strength, Perception, Endurance, Charisma, Intelligence, Agility, and Luck (S.P.E.C.I.A.L.). Each of the attributes is given a brief description.

Strength

"S is for Strength, and that means I am strong! I can carry lots of toys and swing stuff all day long!" Raw physical strength. A high Strength is good for physical characters. Strength is a measure of your raw physical power. It affects how much you can carry, and determines the effectiveness of all melee attacks.

Perception

"P is for Perception, a long funny word! It means what I tasted, smell, saw and heard!" The ability to see, hear, taste and notice unusual things. A high Perception is important for a sharpshooter. Perception is your environmental awareness and "sixth sense," your ability to detect stealthy movement, and affects weapon accuracy in V.A.T.S.

Endurance

"E is for Endurance, and that's how long I can play! I'm always really healthy, and have energy all day!" Stamina and physical toughness. A character with a high Endurance will survive where others may not. Endurance is the measure of overall physical fitness. It affects your total health, your resistance to disease, and the action point drain from sprinting.

Charisma

"C is for Charisma, it's why people think I'm great! I make my friends all laugh and smile, and never want to hate!" A combination of appearance and charm. A high Charisma is important for characters that want to influence people with words. Charisma is your ability to charm and convince others. It affects your success to persuade others in dialogue and prices when you barter. Charisma is your ability to lead and help others. It allows you to share higher point Perk Cards and also affects your rewards from Group Missions and prices when you barter.

Intelligence

"I is for Intelligence, it means I'm really smart! I use my brain for lots of stuff, like science, math and art!" Knowledge, wisdom and the ability to think quickly. A high Intelligence is important for any character. Intelligence is the measure of your overall mental acuity, and affects the number of experience points earned. Intelligence is a measure of your overall mental acuity. It affects the Condition and Durability of crafted items, the amount of material gained from Scrapping, and the difficulty of Hacking.

Agility

"A is for Agility, that's how I get around! I move real fast and easy, and I never make a sound!" Coordination and the ability to move well. A high Agility is important for any active character. Agility is a measure of your overall finesse and reflexes. It affects the number of Action Points in V.A.T.S. and your ability to sneak.

Luck

"L is for Luck, and it's simple, you see! It means that good things always happen to me!" Fate. Karma. An extremely high or low Luck will affect the character - somehow. Events and situations will be changed by how lucky (or unlucky) your character is. Luck is a measure of your general good fortune, durability of items that the player loots, and affects the recharge rates of critical hits.
