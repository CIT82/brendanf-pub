Our Story

One of our greatest creations was the Vault network project. The US government started Project Safehouse in 2054 to fund the creation of shelters that would protect the American population in the event of a nuclear war or plague. We accepted this great task with breakthroughs in construction techniques that allow for these gargantuan underground, bunkers to be constructed at a rapid pace.
